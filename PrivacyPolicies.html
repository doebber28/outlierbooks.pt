Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Outlier Books collects, uses, and shares your information. By using our application, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Data Collection & Authentication (Supabase)

We use Supabase as our backend service provider for authentication and database management:

  • Account Data: We collect your email address to create and manage your secure user profile.
  • Data Security: Your data is stored in secure servers using industry-standard encryption protocols (HTTPS/SSL).

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

To keep our services free, we display advertisements provided by Google AdMob:

  • Identifiers: Google may use unique device identifiers (such as IDFA on iOS or AAID on Android) to serve personalized ads based on your interests.
  • Tracking Transparency (iOS): On Apple devices, we fully respect your choice regarding the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. If declined, only non-personalized ads will be shown.
  • Third-Party Policy: For more details, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Deletion Rights

In compliance with global privacy regulations (GDPR/LGPD), we ensure your right to data removal:

  • You can permanently delete your account at any time through the Settings menu within the app.
  • Upon confirmation, all authentication data and associated records linked to your User ID in Supabase will be permanently erased from our servers.
